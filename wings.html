Wings Recipe

Description

Tender hickory-smoked chicken wings without the wait.

Ingredients

  • 4 pounds frozen chicken wings
  • 1 tps paprika
  • 1 tps chilli powder
  • 1 tps onion powder
  • 1/4 tps cayenne pepper
  • 1/8 tps salt
  • 1/8 tps ground black pepper
  • barbeque sauce

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 175 C.
  2. Place chicken wings on a baking sheet.
  3. Bake in the preheated oven for 1 hour.
  4. Combine paprika, chili powder, onion powder, cayenne pepper, salt, and black pepper in a small bowl. Coat baked chicken wings with the dry rub. Place wings in the smoker with the hickory wood chips.
  5. Smoke chicken for 1 hour at 225 degrees F (110 degrees C).
  6. Transfer smoked wings to a large bowl and toss with barbeque sauce.
